# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone. We expect all participants to uphold the following standards:

**Expected behavior**

- Use clear, professional, and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Prioritize the well-being of the community over individual interests
- Show empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Publishing private information of others without explicit permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with this repository. It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

The project maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors deemed inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.
